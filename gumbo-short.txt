GUMBO RECIPE
 
Ingredients (NOT SO Heart Healthy Version)
1. Enough flour to make a roux
2. 1/2 cup of oil 
3. Celery
4. Bell Pepper
5. Onion
6. Chicken broth
7. Andouille sausage 
8. Rotisserie chicken

Steps
1. Make a Roux
2. Chop the veggies.
3. Brown the sausage.
4. Add to a large pot.
5. Add meat.
6. Serve over hot cooked rice.
